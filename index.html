Our Courses

Animation

Step into the world of imagination and movement! Our Animation course teaches you how to create life-like characters, captivating stories, and smooth motion using industry tools like Adobe Animate, Blender, and Maya. Learn both 2D and 3D animation, storyboarding.

Video Editing

Transform raw footage into powerful stories! In this course, you’ll master editing techniques, color correction, audio design, and transitions using professional software such as Adobe Premiere Pro and DaVinci Resolve.

Graphic Design

Design visuals that inspire and communicate! Our Graphic Design course covers everything from typography, branding, and composition to digital illustration and layout design. Learn tools like Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator.

Motion Graphic Design

Learn how to combine graphics, movement, and sound to create dynamic visual stories. Using tools like After Effects and Cinema 4D, you’ll craft animated infographics, explainer videos.

Generative AI

Step into the future of creativity with Generative AI! Discover how artificial intelligence can help you generate images, videos, music, and design concepts instantly. Learn to use advanced tools like Midjourney, Runway ML.

VFX

This course covers the exciting world of visual effects for film, TV, and digital media. Learn key techniques like green screen compositing, particle simulation, rotoscoping, and CGI integration using tools like After Effects, Nuke, and Houdini.

Game Design

Turn your ideas into playable experiences! Our Game Design course teaches the full process of creating engaging games—from concept and storytelling to level design, 3D modeling, and gameplay mechanics.
